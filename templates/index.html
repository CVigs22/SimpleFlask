Malcom Gladwell

Author, Journalist, Podcaster, and Public Speaker


An illustration of Malcolm Gladwell courtesy of The New Yorker.

As a staff writer for the New York Times since 1996, Malcolm Gladwell has written several best-sellers that include The Tipping Point: How Little Things Can Make A Big Difference, Blink: The Power of Thinking Without Thinking, and many more. Each of his 5 books have found a home on the New York Time's Bestseller's List and deal with implications relating to psychology, sociology, and everyday life
